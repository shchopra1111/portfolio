THE MINIMO
a modular shelf
The Minimo, a modular shelving unit designed to expand storage and add color in a space according to an individual's needs. Minimo, an italian word for
minimal. As the name suggests it has a minimalist approach which is incorporated with a modern design-style. The Minimo consists of a grid-like wall-
board.The intersecting lines of the grid o ers best visual interest and can also be rearranged according to one’s preferences.They are made up of a light-
weight metal to give the design a sleek and modern look. This display unit also comprises small plugs to dangle some of your showpieces. The plugs have a
small stopper to stop the things from falling down. This customisable piece of display is easy to install, easy to handle and easy to fashion.

MATERIAL BOARD
A Boho Chic design draws inspiration from the
carefree, bohemian mystique of a world traveler.
This casual style exudes the eclectic vibes of col-
lected treasures, blending old and new to create a
unique, lived-in look.
An empty corner made into a lounging area, stairs
on the side, a casual personal place to read books
or to spend some personal time, has soft and
rough textures with golden accents. Fills up space
with plants and succulents help bring the out-
doors in and creates a calming aesthetic in your
space.
Materials used- faux fur in the throw blanket,
wood in the ooring and cupboard,faux leather in
the armchair, green velvet cushions and golden
accents.

men’s area is authentic tradititional

colored polo shirts. We have a beautiful
lounging area to relax and a bar to get
some snack while lounging.
